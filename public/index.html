Section 1

Overview

We develops and operates virtual reality applications and related services for Meta platforms, including Meta Quest and Horizon platform integrations. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data and platform data we process, how we use it, when we share it, how long we retain it, and how users can request deletion or exercise other rights.

This policy applies to our applications, games, multiplayer services, support operations, cloud-connected features, and commerce functionality that reference or link to this page.

Important: Meta requires a privacy policy that clearly explains what user data you process, how you process it, why you process it, and how users can request deletion. Keep this page aligned with what your app actually does.
Section 2

Data we process

Depending on the features enabled in the app, we may process the following categories of information:

Profile and identity dataMeta username, user ID where available through platform tools, avatar-related data, and basic profile display information used to identify players in the app.
Friends and social dataFriends eligible for invitation, friend-based leaderboard context, invitation routing data, room membership, and party participation data.
Multiplayer session dataMatchmaking status, room/session identifiers, peer networking state, gameplay synchronization data, and connectivity metadata required to run online sessions.
Voice dataLive microphone audio transmitted during in-app voice chat sessions when users choose to use voice communication features.
Progression dataAchievements, scores, leaderboard entries, cloud save data, unlocked content, settings, and gameplay progress.
Commerce dataPurchase status, entitlement records, downloadable content ownership, in-app purchase restoration data, and custom item ownership where supported.
Technical dataDevice, connection, crash, diagnostic, anti-abuse, and service reliability information reasonably necessary to operate and secure the app.
Support dataAny information you provide when contacting us for customer support, moderation, or privacy requests.
Section 3

How we use data

We use personal data and platform data only as needed to operate the app and the features users access.

Social and multiplayer features

  • To create and manage multiplayer rooms, place users into matches, and maintain live session state.
  • To display usernames, avatars, friends eligible for invitation, and friend-based leaderboards.
  • To allow users to invite others, join parties, use party chat continuity, and communicate through VoIP.
  • To support peer-to-peer networking and other real-time multiplayer synchronization functions.

Gameplay and persistence

  • To record achievements, preserve cloud saves, store settings, and maintain progression across sessions or devices.
  • To deliver custom items for supported Meta Home environments and maintain ownership state for earned or purchased content.

Commerce and support

  • To process in-app purchases, authenticate add-ons and downloadable content, restore entitlements, and prevent purchase fraud.
  • To respond to user support requests, enforce our rules, diagnose service issues, and comply with applicable legal obligations.
Section 4

How we share information

We may share information in the following limited situations:

  • With Meta platform services: when necessary to use Meta platform APIs, social features, commerce tools, cloud services, achievements, avatar systems, and similar platform functionality.
  • With service providers: where vendors help us host infrastructure, provide customer support, detect fraud, or operate backend systems on our behalf and subject to contractual restrictions.
  • For legal and safety reasons: when required by law, legal process, or valid public authority request, or when necessary to protect users, rights, property, or platform security.
  • In business transfers: if our company is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale, subject to applicable law.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not process Meta-related user data for purposes that are inconsistent with this policy.

Section 5

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain multiplayer and account-linked features, restore purchases, preserve cloud saves, investigate abuse, resolve disputes, and satisfy legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the data type, whether the feature remains enabled, whether a deletion request has been submitted, and what applicable law requires. When information is no longer needed, we delete it or de-identify it where appropriate.

Section 6

User rights and deletion requests

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we control by contacting us at [elcast@outlook.cz]. If your request relates to data primarily controlled by Meta through your account or device settings, Meta may also provide separate privacy controls.

To help us process a request, please include your app name, your Meta username or account identifier relevant to the request, a description of the issue, and the action you want us to take. We may ask for reasonable verification before fulfilling certain requests.

Section 7

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we aim to use safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data we process.

Section 8

Children and age-appropriate use

Our app is intended only for users who are permitted to use the service under applicable law and Meta platform rules. If we learn that we have collected personal data in a manner not permitted by applicable law, we will take appropriate action, including deletion where required.
